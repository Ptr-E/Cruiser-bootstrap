Our Clothing

We are a small, independent clothing company started by two friends on the beautiful NorCal Coast.
Our apparel is made for those who seek out to live the Cruiser Lifestyle, that is, to live by thier own rules and own terms. Who work hard and play hard. Cruiser Gear comes in a number of styles and colors that will fit any event or activity for the everyday individual. Our clothes are made of the highest quality, thinkness, durability and can take whatever you dish out.
Made in the USA.
